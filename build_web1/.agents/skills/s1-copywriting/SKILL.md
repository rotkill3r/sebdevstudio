# Marketing Skill: Copywriting

## What it does
Writes and optimizes all copy for Seb_DevStudio landing pages, service descriptions, CTAs, and email sequences.

## Tone
Technical but accessible. Developer-first. Clear, concise, no fluff.

## Key Pages
- Hero section value proposition
- Service card descriptions
- Wizard step explanations
- FAQ answers
- Contact form copy

## Best Practices
- Lead with "what you get", not "how it works"
- Use concrete numbers (3 services, 5 agents, $800)
- Avoid jargon unless the audience expects it
- Every CTA should state the action and the outcome
