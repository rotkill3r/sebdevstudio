# Seb_DevStudio — Product Marketing Context

## Product
**Seb_DevStudio** is a development service that uses AI subagents to build web projects. Three service tiers let clients choose their level of involvement.

## Target Audience
- Founders and non-technical entrepreneurs needing MVPs/prototypes
- Businesses wanting modern web presence (landing pages, web apps)
- Tech teams needing architecture consulting or migration plans

## Value Proposition
"3 servicios de desarrollo con subagentes de IA. Precio transparente, entregas rápidas, sin rodeos."

## Key Differentiators
- **5 subagentes** instead of a single black-box AI
- **3 service tiers** from rapid prototype to full consulting
- **Transparent pricing** per project type, not hourly
- **4-phase diagnostic wizard** that recommends the right service

## Voice & Tone
Technical but accessible. Developer-first language. Clear, concise, no fluff.
